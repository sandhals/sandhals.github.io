I've moved cities several times in the past few years. This has meant having to leave most of my books in Canada in big cardboard boxes, and doing most of my reading now on a Kindle. Even so, I couldn't leave every book behind, especially books that I don't own digitally. And living here and there, browsing bookshops, sometimes I can't help but purchase a physical book. This page tracks the state of my humble, current bookshelf.
